Diversity in STEM Matters

STEM drives innovation for everyone, but such innovation would not be possible without a diversity in perspectives. This lack of diversity represents a loss of talent and discovery, and thus, hinders further societal development. From a logistical standpoint, studies also show that companies with diverse workforces outperform others financially, with companies in the top 25% for gender diversity being 15% more likely to have higher financial returns.
